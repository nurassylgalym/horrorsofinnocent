a few words from the creators

We are for freedom! For freedom of speech, for freedom of right and choice!

You probably have a question, why did a group of ordinary teenagers from the CIS countries suddenly decide to make such a complex website in terms of semantic load? My idea was to show how wars affect the most ordinary people and that they bring with them only chaos, which gives rise to pain, anger and suffering. There are no benefits from wars. Do you think the winner will win a ton of money in reparations? But have you taken into account how many victims were killed? No economic excuse will work as a reason for war. Is it worth tens, hundreds, thousands, tens of thousands, hundreds of thousands and sometimes even a million lives for this?

Among these people were fathers and mothers, brothers and sisters, uncles and aunts, grandparents.

We, as residents of countries without military conflicts (as of September 2023, Kazakhstan is not at war) do not pay attention to wars in other countries.

We greatly underestimate peacetime and freedom. We want to convey through this site that people must value their lives. All the best and peace. Assalamualaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh!


- Nurassyl Galym
